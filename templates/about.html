    About Carparks SG

    About Carparks SG

    Carparks SG contains information accessed on {{ date }} from Carpark Availability dataset from LTA DataMall, Carpark Availability dataset from GovTech, and Carpark Available Lots dataset from URA which are made available under the terms of the Singapore Open Data Licence version 1.0

    Scratching your head trying to mentally calculate parking charges? Shocked at the price when you exit the carpark gantry? Trying to guesstimate where the cheapest carpark might be?

    No more - Carparks SG helps you save money by taking the guesswork out of parking.

    With Carparks SG, you can:
    • find the cheapest carparks for a given location and duration(s)
    • compare prices and distance from given location of the results
    • search for carparks around a location
    • see how much you're going to pay in advance
    • compare prices with nearby carparks
    • see parking rates and other information
    • see number of available lots in real-time where available
    • get directions to the carpark
